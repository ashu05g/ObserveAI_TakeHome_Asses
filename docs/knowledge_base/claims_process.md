# Claims Process

## How to file a claim

A new claim can be filed through any of these channels:

- **Online**: claims.observeinsurance.com (fastest — typical filing time ~10 minutes)
- **Phone**: our claims hotline (24/7)
- **In person**: any regional office during business hours
- **Through your independent agent** (if you have one)

Filing a claim requires:
- Policy number (printed on your ID card or in the customer portal)
- Date and approximate time of the incident
- A short description of what happened
- Contact information for any other parties involved
- Photos or supporting documentation (uploadable to the portal)

## Claim lifecycle

Every claim moves through these stages:

1. **Filed** — claim is intake into our system, assigned a claim ID (`CLM-YYYY-NNNN`), and routed to an adjuster.
2. **Under review** — the assigned adjuster collects information, may request additional documentation, and contacts the policyholder if anything is unclear.
3. **Assessment** — for property/auto claims, an inspection may be scheduled (in-person, virtual, or photo-based depending on the type).
4. **Decision** — the adjuster issues a decision: approved, partially approved, denied, or requires-documentation.
5. **Payout** (for approved claims) — payment is issued to the policyholder or directly to a repair shop / medical provider per the policyholder's election.

## Typical timelines

- **Auto claims (simple)**: decision within 7–10 business days; payout within 3–5 business days after approval.
- **Auto claims (complex, e.g., disputed liability or total loss)**: 2–6 weeks.
- **Home claims**: decision within 10–15 business days; longer if a contractor estimate is required.
- **Health (supplemental) claims**: decision within 14 business days of receiving all required documentation.
- **Life claims**: 30–60 days for term/whole life; longer if contestability period applies (within first 2 years of policy).

## Claim statuses

- **Approved** — the claim has been decided in the policyholder's favor; payment is being processed or has been issued.
- **Pending** — the claim is under active review by the adjuster.
- **Requires documentation** — the adjuster has requested specific items from the policyholder; the claim cannot proceed until those are received.

## Submitting requested documentation

When a claim is in "requires_documentation" status, the requested items can be submitted via:

- **Secure portal**: portal.observeinsurance.com (preferred — fastest processing)
- **Email**: support@observeinsurance.com — always include the claim ID in the subject line
- **Mail**: 100 Insurance Plaza, San Francisco, CA 94105, attn: Claims Documentation

Once documentation is received, the typical re-review window is **3–5 business days**.

## Working with your adjuster

Each claim is assigned a single adjuster who is the policyholder's primary point of contact. The adjuster's name and direct phone are listed in the claim summary in the customer portal. Adjusters typically respond to messages within one business day.

## Appeals

If a claim is denied or only partially approved, the policyholder may appeal within **30 days** of the decision letter. Appeals are reviewed by a senior adjuster (independent of the original decision-maker) and resolved within 21 business days.

## Escalation

If the policyholder is not satisfied with the handling of a claim, they may escalate to:
1. The adjuster's supervisor (request via the adjuster directly).
2. Our Office of the Customer Advocate (independent group): advocate@observeinsurance.com.
3. State Department of Insurance — Observe will provide the relevant filing information on request.

## Payment methods

Approved claim payouts can be sent via:
- Direct deposit (ACH) — usually within 2–3 business days
- Mailed check — usually within 5–7 business days
- Direct payment to a repair shop or medical provider (when authorized)
