# Customer Service

## How to reach us

- **Main customer service line**: available Monday through Friday, 8 AM to 6 PM Eastern Time
- **24-hour emergency line**: 1-800-555-0911 (use this only for accidents in progress, theft in progress, or urgent property loss)
- **Online portal**: portal.observeinsurance.com (24/7)
- **Email**: support@observeinsurance.com (typical response within 1 business day)
- **Mailing address**: 100 Insurance Plaza, San Francisco, CA 94105

## What can be done in the portal

The customer portal (portal.observeinsurance.com) supports:
- Viewing policy details and ID cards
- Filing a new claim
- Uploading documents to an existing claim
- Viewing claim status and adjuster contact info
- Making a one-time payment or updating autopay
- Updating contact information and beneficiaries
- Requesting a quote for additional coverage
- Downloading tax documents (Form 1099 for life insurance distributions)

## Languages supported

Phone support is available in English and Spanish during regular business hours. Other languages (Mandarin, Vietnamese, Tagalog) are available through our interpreter line — request a translator when calling and one will be added within a few minutes.

## Accessibility

- TTY/TDD line available for hearing-impaired customers
- Large-print policy documents available on request
- Screen-reader-compatible portal (WCAG 2.1 AA compliant)

## When to escalate to a human

Even when self-service is available, callers should be transferred to a human representative for:
- Disputes about claim decisions
- Complex policy questions involving multiple coverages
- Reports of suspected fraud
- Requests to add or remove a driver/beneficiary involving legal documentation
- Emotional or sensitive situations (e.g., recent loss, accident with injuries)

## Office hours

- **Claims team**: Monday through Friday, 8 AM to 6 PM Eastern Time
- **Customer service**: Monday through Friday, 8 AM to 8 PM Eastern Time; Saturday 9 AM to 5 PM ET
- **Sales / new quotes**: Monday through Friday, 8 AM to 7 PM ET
- **Emergency line**: 24/7
- **Closed**: New Year's Day, Memorial Day, Independence Day, Labor Day, Thanksgiving, Christmas Day

## Privacy and security

Observe Insurance never asks for full Social Security numbers, full credit card numbers, or account passwords via phone or unsolicited email. If a caller receives a suspicious communication claiming to be from Observe, they can report it to fraud@observeinsurance.com.
