# About Observe Insurance

Observe Insurance is a multi-line carrier headquartered in San Francisco, California, serving over 1.2 million policyholders across all 50 states. Founded in 1987 as a regional auto insurer, the company expanded into home, health, and life insurance through the 1990s and 2000s.

## Mission

To make insurance simple, transparent, and human — by combining straightforward policy language, fast claims handling, and a 24-hour support model that respects our customers' time.

## Lines of business

- **Auto insurance** — personal, commercial, and rideshare endorsements
- **Home insurance** — homeowners, condo, and renters
- **Health insurance** — supplemental health plans (not a primary medical insurer)
- **Life insurance** — term and whole life

## Customer base

We serve individuals, families, and small businesses. Roughly 65% of our policyholders are multi-policy customers (e.g., both auto and home), and the average customer tenure is 8.3 years.
