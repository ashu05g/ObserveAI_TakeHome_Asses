# Policies and Coverage

## Auto insurance

**Coverage types included by default:**
- Bodily injury liability — $100,000 per person / $300,000 per accident
- Property damage liability — $50,000 per accident
- Uninsured/underinsured motorist coverage — matches liability limits
- Personal injury protection (PIP) — $10,000 default
- Medical payments — $5,000 default

**Optional coverage:**
- Comprehensive (theft, vandalism, weather, animal collision)
- Collision (at-fault and not-at-fault collisions)
- Roadside assistance
- Rental reimbursement (up to $40/day, 30-day max)
- Rideshare endorsement (for Uber/Lyft drivers)

**Standard deductibles:**
- Comprehensive: $250, $500 (most common), or $1,000
- Collision: $500 (most common), $1,000, or $1,500

## Home insurance

**Standard coverage:**
- Dwelling — replacement cost of the home
- Other structures (detached garage, shed) — 10% of dwelling
- Personal property — 50–70% of dwelling, actual-cash-value or replacement-cost
- Loss of use — 20% of dwelling
- Personal liability — $300,000 default, raisable to $500K or $1M
- Medical payments to others — $5,000

**Standard deductibles:**
- $500, $1,000 (most common), $2,500, or 1% of dwelling value

**Common exclusions:**
- Flood (separate NFIP policy required)
- Earthquake (separate endorsement)
- Routine wear and tear
- Intentional damage
- Business activities on the premises (unless endorsed)

## Health insurance (supplemental)

We offer **supplemental** plans only — these complement a primary medical insurer (e.g., your employer plan or ACA marketplace plan). They are **not** primary medical coverage.

**Plan types:**
- Accident indemnity (lump-sum payouts for covered injuries)
- Critical illness (lump-sum for diagnoses like cancer, stroke, heart attack)
- Hospital indemnity (daily benefit for inpatient stays)

**Typical caps:**
- Per-event maximums: $5,000–$50,000 depending on plan tier
- Annual maximums: $25,000–$250,000

## Life insurance

**Term life:**
- Terms: 10, 15, 20, 25, or 30 years
- Face amounts: $50,000 to $5,000,000
- Convertible to whole life within the first 10 years of the term

**Whole life:**
- Face amounts: $25,000 to $1,000,000
- Cash value accumulation
- Loan provisions against cash value (after year 3)

## Policy renewal and cancellation

- Policies auto-renew unless the policyholder cancels.
- Renewal notices are sent **45 days** before the renewal date.
- Cancellation can be done online, by phone, or in writing.
- Pro-rated refunds are issued for unused premium when canceling mid-term.
- A 30-day grace period applies for missed premium payments before lapse.

## Premium changes

Renewal premiums may change based on claim history, credit-based insurance score (where allowed), driving record (auto), home characteristics (home), and regulatory filings. Customers receive at least 45 days notice of any renewal premium change.
